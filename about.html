About Our School

School History

Founded in 1990, Greenwood International School started as a small initiative to provide quality education. Over the last three decades, we have grown into a premier institution, shaping the lives of thousands of students with a blend of traditional values and modern teaching methodologies.

Mission

To empower students to acquire, demonstrate, articulate, and value knowledge and skills that will support them as life-long learners.

Vision

To be a global leader in education, nurturing students to become responsible, ethical, and compassionate citizens.

Values

Integrity, Excellence, Respect, Responsibility, and Compassion form the bedrock of our community.

Principal's Message

"Welcome to Greenwood. We are committed to providing a safe and intellectually challenging environment that will empower students to become innovative thinkers."


- Dr. Sarah Jenkins, Principal

Accreditations

Affiliated with the International School Board (ISB) and accredited by the Global Education Council.
